About

Code for America is hosting an evening event with leaders in the government technology field in the Bay Area. On November 3rd in San Francisco Code for America is hosting the Bay Area Government Technology Showcase. The Showcase will feature alumni from our 2014 Incubator and Accelerator Programs, as well as other individuals actively working and investing in the government technology ecosystem.

Refreshments at 6pm, speakers at 7pm.
