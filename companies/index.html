Accelerator

The Code for America Accelerator “turbo-charges” select govtech startups by providing them an opportunity to amplify market awareness of their product, to access a wealth of business training and advice, and to be introduced to a broad network of potential investors and government leaders. The program runs from July to October and those chosen receive $25K in funding, mentorship from celebrated govtech veterans and access to the Code for America network.

  • Learn more

Incubator

The CfA Incubator is a no-nonsense, highly focused experience helping teams build a sustainable business from their current product ideas. The program runs from December to June to support the sustainability of applications emerging from the fellowship, to develop apps into scalable business enterprises, and to retain these talented govtech technologists where they are most needed: strengthening the ecosystem.

  • Learn more

Mentors

The mentors in the companies program are leaders in the technology and public administration space on a variety of topics, such as customer research, entrepreneurship, finance, legal, marketing, product, sales, strategy, UX and venture capital.
