Becca Blazak

Fellowship Program Coordinator

Member of

  • Staff

Becca joined Code for America in May 2014. As Fellowship Program Coordinator, Becca works on all aspects of program developemnt, helping to streamline processes around mentorship, personal and professional developement, and fellowship recruitment. Prior to joining Code for America, Becca worked in a similar role at the Skoll Foundation where she provided support and helped develop projects such as the Young Leader Initiative and Corporate Experience for the 2014 Skoll World Forum. Before moving to the Bay Area, Becca spent 15 months teaching English in South Korea and backpacking around Southeast Asia. While in South Korea, she was the co-creator of an after school English program for elementary aged girls at Girls Town Orphanage. Becca holds a B.A. in Culture and Communications with a focus in International Studies and a minor in Politics from Ithaca College.
