Matt Boitano

Grants Administrator

Member of

  • Staff

Matt supports CfA's relationships with institutional supporters and local match funders, while implementing more efficient internal processes and workflows. He is an Oakland native with a passion for civic engagement, technology and inclusion. Previously, he wore multiple hats at Oakland Local, providing support for its Development department while spearheading Outreach for its award-winning Hack the Hood program. He has significant experience in the nonprofit world, having worked at a variety of organizations including California Certified Organic Farmers (CCOF) and Committee to Bridge the Gap. He graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of California, Santa Cruz.
