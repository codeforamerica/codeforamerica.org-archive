Matt Boitano

Development Coordinator

Member of

  • Staff

Matt (Development Coordinator) works on the Partnerships Team, helping to support CfA's relationships with institutional supporters, corporate sponsors, individual donors and local match funders. He is an Oakland native with a passion for civic engagement in tech and making government run more efficiently. Previously, he wore multiple hats at Oakland Local, providing support for its Development department while spearheading Outreach for its Hack the Hood program. He has significant experience in the nonprofit world, having worked at a variety of organizations including California Certified Organic Farmers (CCOF) and Committee to Bridge the Gap. He graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of California, Santa Cruz. When he’s not coding for America, Matt enjoys podcasts, chess and fantasy baseball.
