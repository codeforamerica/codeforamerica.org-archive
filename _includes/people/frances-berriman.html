Frances Berriman

Head of Digital Front Door

Member of

Frances Berriman is a British developer who relocated from London to San Francisco in the summer of 2013, joining Code for America in January 2014. She previously worked as the front-end lead for GOV.UK, the UK Government Digital Service's award-winning flagship project that formed a key part of the digital strategy overhaul of services for citizens in the UK. She heads up the Digital Front Door intiative, and is the Interim Director of the Communications and Engagement Focus Area (and is seeking someone to fill this role permanently).
