Jeff Reichman

Member of

  • Houston, TX Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Jeff is a writer, entrepreneur, musician, and map nerd. He runs January Advisors, an innovation consulting firm that he founded in 2009. In that role, he works with startups, large corporations, and the City of Houston to help develop new products, services, and communities. Jeff also represents Houston with Startup America, co-organizes Open Houston and Startup Softball, curates the community blog Startup Houston, and is a director of Bayou Microfund, a Houston-focused, Kiva-backed non-profit.
