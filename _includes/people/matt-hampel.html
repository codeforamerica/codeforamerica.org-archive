Matt Hampel

Member of

  • 2012 Fellows

Matt Hampel is a web developer and student of civic information. Matt has worked with nonprofits, newspapers, universities, and other organizations to build tools for the public good. Most recently, he worked as a technology project manager at the University of Michigan. He has developed multiple open data web apps, including DetroitLedger.com for Detroit-related civic information and A2Docs.org for collecting public documents from Ann Arbor, MI.
