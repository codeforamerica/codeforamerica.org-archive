Bill Bushey

Member of

  • Twin Cities, MN Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Bill is a civic nerd – a software developer who works where technology meets society. Bill currently develops software, provides technology guidance, and plans technology initiatives as the Technology Coordinator for E-Democracy.org. Bill is also a co-organizer of Open Twin Cities, the Code for America Brigade serving Minneapolis/St. Paul, Minnesota. Prior to joining E-Democracy and Open Twin Cities, Bill received a B.S. in Computer Science from Clarkson University and an M.S. in Science, Technology, and Environmental Policy from the Humphrey School of Public Affairs. He has developed software and provided technical consultation for Thomson Reuters, IBM, and Harvard's Berkman Center for Internet and Society, and has participated in Google's Summer of Code.
