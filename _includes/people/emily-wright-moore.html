Emily Wright Moore

Member of

  • 2012 Fellows

Emily Wright Moore is a designer and graphic artist focused on usability and visual storytelling. Emily has worked for several Bay Area groups including Banned by the Bay, Red Ink Studios, and Intersection for the Arts, where she researched and co-designed Golden Rules as part of the Prison Project. In 2006, she completed her BFA in Illustration at the California College of Arts.
