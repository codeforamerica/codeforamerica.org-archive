Andrea Hansen

Member of

  • 2014 Fellows

Andrea is an optimistic urbanist, data visualizer, and cartography devotee. Her practice, Fluxscape, focuses on data-driven landscapes in post-industrial cities, and she also runs Visualizing Systems, an online catalog that investigates how we map data to understand complex networks. She teaches these concepts in the landscape architecture program at Harvard University’s Graduate School of Design, where she was the 2011-2012 Daniel Urban Kiley Fellow.
