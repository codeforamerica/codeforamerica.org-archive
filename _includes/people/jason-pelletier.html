Jason Pelletier

Member of

  • Burlington, VT Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Jason Pelletier is Co-Founder and Creative Director at Found Line, a creative services studio with design and development capabilities in web, print, and mobile. Found Line clients include Vermont Public Radio, Fletcher Allen Health Care, CCTV Center for Media & Democracy, and ThinkFlood. Prior to Found Line, he worked within the Creative Services teams at Stonyfield Farm and Seventh Generation. He is a founding member and co-organizer of Code for BTV, a Code for America Brigade. As a co-organizer of BTV Gig, he co-authored Burlington's Gigabit Opportunity report. He also co-organizes Vermont Code Camp.
