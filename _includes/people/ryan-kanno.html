Ryan Kanno

Member of

  • Hawaii Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Ryan Kanno is Co-Captain of Code for Hawaii. He's spent over 15 years as a software developer / manager in the defense, media, and finance industries. Prior to leading Code for Hawaii, he's helped organize HI Capacity, the first Honolulu Makerspace. Currently, he is the Ansible Hawaiʻi Meetup Organizer.

Ryan has a BS in Computer Science from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, a Master's in Computer Science from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, and a Master's in Business Administration from the University of Hawaii.
