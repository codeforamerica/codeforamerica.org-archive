Chacha Sikes

Member of

  • 2011 Fellows

Chacha Sikes is an experienced developer who builds organizational websites and participatory educational experiences. She was a core developer for the state of Missouri’s Department of Conservation website. She also founded the Drupal Open Garden Project, a collaborative learning project that brings technologists and community gardeners together to work on open source technology for local food systems. She holds a BA in History and Anthropology from Bard College.
