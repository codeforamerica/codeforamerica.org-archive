Aaron Ogle

Member of

  • 2011 Fellows

Aaron believes that software can be used to build for a better world, and he loves designing applications to make cities more livable, beautiful, and just. While a fellow at Code for America, he partnered with the City of Philadelphia to build solutions to help foster civic engagement. He specializes in JavaScript and GIS development and has contributed to such applications as reroute.it, septa.mobi, changeby.us, walkshed.org, and phillystormwater.org. He is now CTO at OpenPlans. Besides a tech geek, Aaron is an outdoorsman, an urbanist, and a devoted husband and father.
