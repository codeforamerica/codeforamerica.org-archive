Lou Huang

Member of

  • 2013 Fellows

Lou is a front-end web developer and LEED-accredited urban designer based in San Francisco. A recovering architect, he has worked on projects from coffee shops to master plans for mixed-income residential neighborhoods, as well as guidelines and plans for a variety of cities. During the fellowship, he developed a variety of projects for Las Vegas, as well as Streetmix and NAICS API. He holds a BA in Architecture from University of California Berkeley and a Master of City Planning from the University of Pennsylvania.
