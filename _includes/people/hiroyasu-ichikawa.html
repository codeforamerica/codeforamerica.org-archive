Hiroyasu Ichikawa

Member of

  • Brigade Captains

Hiroyasu Ichikawa is a social media consultant focusing on the innovative use of social media for empowering social entrepreneurship through researching, writing and public speaking for Corporations, Governments and NPOs. He is a regular columnist on social media for social good trends, featuring broad range of topics and cases, including the role of technology for government, civic participation, disaster preparedness and relief, education, etc... Author of "Social Good Guide". Graduate of Doshisha Univ. and Amherst College.
