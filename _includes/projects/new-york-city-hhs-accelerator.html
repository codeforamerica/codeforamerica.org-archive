New York City: HHS Accelerator

New York launched the HHS Accelerator in 2012 to reduce the complexity and duplication in the procurement process for service providers and agencies working with the Department of Heath and Human Services. The HHS Accelerator developed new business processes and a technical platform, Data Vault, to improve document submission, data managed and collection, and improve government reporting and transparency.
