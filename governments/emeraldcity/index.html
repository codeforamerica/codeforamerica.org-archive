Emerald City

Known for its special glowing beauty, the Emerald City is a beacon for seekers of many kinds of services and benefits. Despite the difficulty of travel, it enjoys significant immigration, and its diverse population of animals and humans participates in one of the world's most innovative green economies.

Led by a ruler who embraces the maker movement fully, the city has begun to open up geo data from its Royal Army's webcams and sensors, although civic hackers are still hoping to get their hands on complete parcel data for its 9,654 jewel-encrusted buildings
