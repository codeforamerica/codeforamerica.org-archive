Indianapolis

Indianapolis is the 13th largest metro area in the nation serving as a central node for national shipping and transportation. In recent years, Indy has endured crime rates as high as three times the national average. This midwestern transit hub will be working with the Code for America fellows to develop tools to improve data analysis, increase transparency and highlight public engagement to improve safety for all its residents.
