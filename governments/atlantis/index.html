Atlantis

Located just beyond the Pillars of Hercules, Atlantis is a community in recovery. A powerful tourism mecca during the Antedeluvian period, the city was once characterized by its maritime character, underwater sea gardens, and of course, its burgeoning spearfishing industry.

No stranger to technology, Atlantis' civic hackers were among the strongest in the world. Today, marred by the de-industrialization and population decline that characterizes so many Rust Belt cities, the community continues to rebuild. With help from the 2015 Code for America Fellows, the community will continue to challenge what media outlets have for too long characterized as a "lost city."
