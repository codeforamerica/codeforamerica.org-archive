Logos

Style Guide

Our logo authenticates the source of an app, site or document and represents a partnership between you and Code for America.

Non-standard versions of the logo confuse its meaning and dilute its power. Please don't alter or change the logo's color or font.

Proper use of CfA's logos are essential in maintaining a clear and consistent brand.

For colors, typography, and web styles you can view our pattern library at style.codeforamerica.org
