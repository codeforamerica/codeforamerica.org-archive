About

OpenTrails (The Open Trail System Specification) is an open data format developed in partnership between Code for America and a broad coalition of park agencies, outdoor software industry members, and non-profit leaders like the National Recreation and Parks Association. OpenTrails allows agencies to publish their data in a common format for use in shared websites and apps, and makes the data open and available for use by third party developers, and large consumers of data.
