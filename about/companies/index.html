Program Overview

Govtech (government technology) companies are those that create technology infrastructure—software and hardware, tools and services—governments can use to manage their internal operations and deliver services to citizens."

Code for America's programs provides govtech companies with funding, mentorship, and the right connections to navigate the government tech ecosystem. The program works to bolster a thriving and competitive marketplace where governments are offered new technology options at good value for taxpayer dollars.

Accelerator

The Code for America Accelerator “turbo-charges” select govtech companies by providing them an opportunity to amplify market awareness of their product, to access a wealth of business training and advice, and to be introduced to a broad network of potential investors and civic leaders. The program runs from July to October and those chosen receive $25K in funding, mentorship from celebrated govtech veterans and access to the Code for America network.

  • Learn more

Incubator

The CfA Incubator is a no-nonsense, highly focused experience helping teams build a sustainable business from their current product ideas. The program runs from December to June to support the sustainability of applications emerging from the fellowship, to develop apps into scalable business enterprises, and to retain these talented govtech technologists where they are most needed: strengthening the ecosystem.

  • Learn more

Mentors

The mentors in our programs are leaders in the technology and public administration space on a variety of topics, such as customer research, entrepreneurship, finance, legal, marketing, product, sales, strategy, UX and venture capital.
