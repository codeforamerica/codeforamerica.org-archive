The Code for America Brigade Goes International

The Code for America Brigade program is a network of people committed to using their voices and hands, in collaboration with local governments, to make their cities better.

In September 2013, Code for America opened its Brigade network to international chapters. Today, there are 23 international chapters in seven countries. Thousands of people around the world are leveraging technology to make their communities better places. They are working in partnership with local governments to open public data, build civic tools, and advocate for open government policies.
