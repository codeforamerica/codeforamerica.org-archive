Invest in the future of Government

Hundreds of individuals have joined together to invest their time, energy, ideas, and money in our fellows and in our mission. Together we are building a movement that changes the way our cities function -- a movement that helps cities to keep pace with their community and interact with residents using new tools and technologies.

Code for America is generously supported by leaders in philanthropy and technology. Their support makes our work possible. It allows for us to connect city governments with web and design talent.

Please join us. Invest in Code of America and help code the next chapter in American history.

If your foundation or company is interested in supporting our work, please contact Ashley Meyers, development and engagement manager, to discuss how you can align your support with a key need of our organization, or to learn more about Code for America. Ashley can be reached at 415-236-3796 or ashley@codeforamerica.org. More information on grants, in-kind donations, and corporate sponsorships can be found here.

Donations to Code for America, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit and nonpartisan organization, are tax deductible.
