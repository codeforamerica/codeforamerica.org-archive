CourthouseUSA, LLC is a public private partnership formed to make current authoritative property data available in open-source standard format. A streamlined scalable ETL process manages the download, conversion and upload of disparate data onto a platform where 3rd party app developers use APIs free of charge, enabling them to write one app that can be applied nationwide. CUSA motivates local governments to provide data on the same schedule as data is published on their own website in the form of free crowd-sourcing apps encouraging citizen involvement and/or a percent of the revenue as data is licensed.
