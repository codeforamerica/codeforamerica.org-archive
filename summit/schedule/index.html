7:30 - 9:00 PM: Ignite CfA! at YBCA

  • Tuesday night, we’ll gather at the YBCA for a favorite annual Summit tradition: Ignite CfA! Never heard of an Ignite? No worries. An Ignite is a story that’s exactly 5 minutes long, told by a speaker who’s working with 20 slides that auto-advance every 15 seconds (ready or not). We assure you, it’s more fun than scary — it’s fast, and the format forces you to really think about what you want to say.
