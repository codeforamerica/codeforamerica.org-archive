This year, we are thrilled to spotlight leaders across the public, private, non-profit and academic sectors who are transforming government for the 21st century. These champions are:

  • Making digital government services more usable and accessible
  • Helping governments and communities collaborate in new ways
  • Transforming the organizational culture and practices of government
  • Applying data-driven, user-centered, and iterative approaches to civic challenges
  • Making real impact in the areas of public safety, criminal justice, economic development, health, transportation, government operations, and more
