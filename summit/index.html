Announcing the 2015 Summit

When: September 30 - October 2     Where: Oakland Marriot City Center

The 2015 Code for America will kick-off September 30 in Oakland, California. For three days, more than 1,300 government leaders, technologists, and community members will take over the Oakland Marriott City Center, to delve into how, together, we can transform government for the 21st Century.
