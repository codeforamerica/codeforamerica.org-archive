Ignite CfA!

WHEN: 8–10pm, Wednesday, September 24
WHERE: Mezzanine, 444 Jessie St.
WHAT: Drinks and fun at Ignite CfA!

A favorite annual Summit tradition. An Ignite is a story that's exactly 5 minutes long, told by a speaker who's working with 20 slides that auto-advance every 15 seconds (ready or not). It's fun, it's fast, and the format forces you to really think about what you want to say.

Talks can be geeky, fanciful, poignant, educational, and/or funny. Maximum fun, minimum time. It's gonna be a blast.

The submission deadline has passed. Accepted speakers will be notified by Friday, September 19, 3pm PDT.

Curious to learn more about Ignite talks? Watch some videos at igniteshow.com and show up early on Wednesday!

(For registered Summit attendees only. The venue's 21+, so bring your ID.)
