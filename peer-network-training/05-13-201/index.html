About

Broadband access is an important issue at the core of civic technology and innovation, and cities like San Leandro, Calif.; Chattanooga, Ten.; and Kansas City, Kan. + Mo. are already undertaking intiatives to roll out broadband internet access citywide. What does the future of broadband access and local government look like? Watch this conversation about broadband access, policy, and the role of municipal government with guest expert Susan Crawford (broadband policy expert, co-director of the Berkman Center at Harvard University, and author of the recent book Captive Audience).
