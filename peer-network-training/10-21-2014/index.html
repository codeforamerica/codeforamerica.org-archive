About

Citygram makes open data from governments meaningful to all by helping them better understand what's happening in their area. With the app, cities can provide their community with relevant and timely information so they can proactively incorporate community feedback, build trust through transparency, and increase civic engagement. Residents can subscribe to neighborhood-specific notifications for topics such as new businesses, land development, building permits, and more. Initially developed with the City of Charlotte, NC, more than 30 cities have expressed interest in deploying Citygram in their area!

This event is hosted by the Code for America Peer Network, CfA's community of innovators working in local government from over thirty cities and counties. Learn more about the Peer Network and how you can get involved here.
